# Seafood Hot Pot Recipe

Making seafood hot pot is actually not complicated. As long as you prepare fresh ingredients and a suitable soup base, you can easily enjoy delicious hot pot at home. Here are detailed steps and tips:

---

### üç≤ **Ingredients Preparation**

#### 1. **Soup Base Options** (choose one):
- **Clear Soup Base**: Water + ginger slices, scallion sections, goji berries, red dates, highlighting the original flavor of seafood.
- **Spicy Soup Base**: Beef tallow hot pot base + dried chili peppers, Sichuan peppercorns, doubanjiang, suitable for those who prefer strong flavors.
- **Tomato Soup Base**: Tomatoes stir-fried until soft, then simmered with broth, sweet and sour to stimulate appetite.
- **Mushroom Soup Base**: Dried shiitake mushrooms, tea tree mushrooms, etc., simmered for a rich and aromatic flavor.

#### 2. **Seafood Ingredients** (mix and match according to preference):
- Shrimp, crab (recommended: flower crab or swimming crab), squid rings, scallops, clams, razor clams
- Fish fillets (bass, sole, and other boneless fish)
- Processed seafood: fish balls, shrimp paste, baby cuttlefish

#### 3. **Side Dishes & Staple Foods**:
- Vegetables: Napa cabbage, crown daisy, enoki mushrooms, tofu, corn, potato slices
- Staple foods: Vermicelli, udon noodles, rice cakes

#### 4. **Dipping Sauce Recipes** (mix and match freely):
- Classic garlic soy sauce: Minced garlic + light soy sauce + sesame oil + cilantro + Thai chili
- Sesame paste sauce: Sesame paste + peanut butter + fermented tofu + Chinese chive flower sauce + scallions
- Oil sauce: Sesame oil + minced garlic + oyster sauce + chili flakes

---

### üë®‚Äçüç≥ **Cooking Steps**

1. **Prepare Seafood**:
   - Trim shrimp whiskers, butterfly cut, and devein. Scrub crabs clean and chop into pieces.
   - Soak shellfish in salt water to purge sand (about 2 hours), scrub repeatedly.
   - Marinate fish fillets with a little salt, cooking wine, and starch for 10 minutes to remove fishy smell and tenderize.

2. **Simmer the Soup Base**:
   - Add water or broth to the pot, add ginger slices, scallion knots, and your chosen soup base ingredients. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 15 minutes to extract flavor.

3. **Cooking Order** (from hard to cook to easy to cook):
   - First add those that take longer: crab, shellfish, fish head/fish bones ‚Üí cook for 5 minutes
   - Then add medium-cooking items: shrimp, squid, fish fillets ‚Üí cook for 1-2 minutes
   - Finally add vegetables, tofu, vermicelli ‚Üí cook until just done

4. **Enjoyment Tips**:
   - Seafood gets tough easily, avoid overcooking! Shrimp is done when it turns red and curls, fish fillets when they turn white.
   - Add soup while eating to maintain sufficient liquid and prevent burning.

### üí° **Key Tips**

‚úÖ **Choose Fresh Ingredients**: Live shrimp and crabs are best. Frozen seafood should be completely thawed and checked for any off odors.
‚úÖ **Deodorizing is Key**: Shellfish must be thoroughly purged of sand; fish fillets marinated with cooking wine and ginger slices are more tender.
‚úÖ **Don't Make the Soup Base Too Salty**: Seafood itself has a salty and fresh flavor, so the soup base seasoning should be light, relying on dipping sauces for flavor.
‚úÖ **Safety First**: Crab and shellfish must be fully cooked before eating to avoid parasite risks.
‚úÖ **Pair with Refreshing Drinks**: Chilled sour plum soup, lemon honey water, or refreshing beer make it even more enjoyable!

### üéâ **Advanced Creative Eating Methods**

- **Thai Tom Yum Seafood Pot**: Use coconut milk + lemongrass + kaffir lime leaves + Thai chili as the soup base, sour and spicy to stimulate appetite.
- **Cheese Foam Pot**: Add a slice of cheese when the soup base is almost done cooking, melted for a rich milky flavor.
- **One Person, One Pot**: Use an induction cooker + small hot pot, each person chooses their own soup base and ingredients, hygienic and free.
